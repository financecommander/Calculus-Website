Compression AI
Technologies

Making frontier AI models deployable on edge devices at a fraction of compute cost through advanced model compression techniques.

The Problem We Solve

Frontier AI models demand enormous computational resources, making them impractical for edge deployment, real-time applications, and resource-constrained environments. The gap between model capability and deployment feasibility limits AI's reach across industries.

Compression AI Technologies bridges this gap with proprietary compression techniques that shrink model size and compute requirements dramatically — without meaningful accuracy loss.

Quantization

Reducing numerical precision of model weights and activations, compressing models up to 4x while preserving inference quality.

Pruning

Removing redundant neurons and connections to create sparse, efficient architectures that run faster with less memory.

Knowledge Distillation

Training smaller "student" models to replicate the capabilities of large "teacher" models, delivering frontier performance in compact packages.

Overview

What Is Compression AI Technologies?

Compression AI Technologies is a deep-technology company incubated through Calculus AI Labs, specializing in AI model compression for edge deployment. Using advanced quantization, pruning, and knowledge distillation techniques, Compression AI Technologies achieves up to 10x model compression with less than 2% accuracy loss — enabling frontier AI models to run on mobile devices, IoT sensors, and embedded systems at a fraction of standard compute cost. The company serves fintech, healthcare, IoT, and autonomous systems industries.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is AI model compression?
AI model compression is the process of reducing the size and computational requirements of artificial intelligence models through techniques such as quantization, pruning, and knowledge distillation — while preserving model accuracy. This enables large AI models to run efficiently on edge devices, mobile phones, and embedded systems.
How does Compression AI Technologies achieve 10x compression?
Compression AI Technologies achieves up to 10x model compression through a proprietary combination of weight quantization (reducing numerical precision), structured pruning (removing redundant neurons and connections), and knowledge distillation (training compact student models from larger teacher models). This multi-technique approach delivers less than 2% accuracy loss.
What industries use Compression AI Technologies?
Compression AI Technologies serves multiple industries including fintech (real-time fraud detection on mobile devices), healthcare (diagnostic AI on medical devices), IoT (intelligent sensor networks with on-device inference), and autonomous systems (low-latency perception models for vehicles, drones, and robotics).
Is Compression AI Technologies part of Calculus?
Yes, Compression AI Technologies was incubated through Calculus AI Labs, the AI research and deep-technology arm of Calculus. Calculus AI Labs is based in Providence, RI and develops breakthrough technologies across AI, quantum computing, and cybersecurity.
