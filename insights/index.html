About Calculus Insights

Calculus Insights is the research and thought leadership division of Calculus, a multi-discipline finance platform headquartered in Boston, Massachusetts. The Insights hub publishes original market research, data-driven analysis, and commentary across the firm's core verticals: private credit and bridge lending, DSCR and SBA loan markets, real estate development, energy project finance, and artificial intelligence in financial services.

Research is produced by the Calculus team, drawing on proprietary data from the firm's direct lending operations, AI Labs product development, and portfolio company performance. Topics include bridge loan market sizing, cap rate analysis, alternative credit scoring methodologies, explainable AI adoption in underwriting, biomass energy project economics, and USDA rural development program trends.

Calculus Insights serves institutional investors, real estate developers, fintech operators, family offices, and research partners seeking differentiated perspectives on private capital markets and emerging financial technology. All publications are accessible through the Calculus website and distributed to the firm's institutional network.

What is Calculus Insights?

Calculus Insights is the research and thought leadership hub of Calculus, covering private credit markets, AI in financial services, real estate development trends, energy project finance, and fintech innovation. Research is published regularly and distributed to the firm's institutional network.

Research is produced by the Calculus team led by founder Sean Grady, drawing on expertise across direct lending operations, AI Labs product development, real estate development, and institutional asset management.
