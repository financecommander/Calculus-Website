Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

Calculus ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website at www.calculusresearch.io (the "Site") or engage with our services. By accessing or using the Site, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of personal information when you interact with our Site or services:

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, phone number, and company or organization name submitted through our contact forms.
  • Project Details: Information about your project, capital requirements, property details, or investment interests that you voluntarily provide when submitting an inquiry.
  • Usage Data: Information automatically collected when you visit the Site, including IP address, browser type, operating system, pages visited, time spent on pages, and referring URLs.
  • Communication Records: Records of correspondence if you contact us via email, phone, or through our online forms.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your inquiries and provide information about our services.
  • To evaluate lending opportunities, investment suitability, and capital deployment possibilities.
  • To improve, maintain, and optimize our website and services.
  • To communicate with you regarding updates, services, and relevant opportunities.
  • To comply with legal obligations, enforce our terms, and protect against fraud or misuse.

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes. We may share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, subpoena, court order, or other legal process, or when we believe disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, your safety, or the safety of others.

  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, reorganization, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

4. Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include the use of encryption for data transmitted through the Site and secure storage for information collected. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our Site may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience. These technologies are used for the following purposes:

  • Essential Functionality: To enable basic Site functionality, such as page navigation and access to secure areas.
  • Analytics: To understand how visitors interact with our Site, which pages are visited most frequently, and to improve Site performance.
  • Form Tracking: Our contact forms are powered by HubSpot, which may set cookies to track form submissions and user interactions for customer relationship management purposes.

You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings. Please note that disabling cookies may affect certain features of the Site.

Our Site uses the following third-party services, each of which has its own privacy policy governing the use of your information:
