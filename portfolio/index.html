Overview

What Is the Calculus Portfolio?

The Calculus portfolio encompasses five portfolio companies and an active real estate development program, representing over $150 million in deployed capital. Managed across three verticals — Calculus AI Labs (AI and deep technology), Private Equity (financial infrastructure), and Real Estate Development (hospitality, multifamily, mixed-use) — the portfolio reflects Calculus's multi-discipline approach to finance and technology. Headquartered in Providence, Rhode Island, Calculus combines direct lending, proprietary AI research, and institutional asset management under one platform.

FAQ

Portfolio Questions

How many companies are in the Calculus portfolio?
The Calculus portfolio includes five portfolio companies and an active real estate development program, representing over $150 million in deployed capital across three verticals: Calculus AI Labs (AI and deep technology), Private Equity (financial infrastructure), and Real Estate Development (hospitality, multifamily, mixed-use).
What is the Calculus AI Labs portfolio?
The Calculus AI Labs portfolio consists of three deep-technology companies: Compression AI Technologies (AI model compression and quantization), Quantum Protocol System (post-quantum cryptographic infrastructure), and Private AI Networks (secure on-premise AI deployment for sensitive data environments).
What is the Calculus private equity portfolio?
The Calculus private equity portfolio includes two fintech infrastructure companies: Digital Money Center (inclusive digital financial infrastructure and mobile money platforms) and Constitutional Tender (sound money and commodity-backed digital instruments).
Does Calculus invest in real estate?
Yes, Calculus actively invests in real estate development through bridge loans, construction financing, and direct development across hospitality, multifamily, and mixed-use asset classes. The real estate program is managed through the Calculus Lending division.
Can I co-invest in Calculus portfolio companies?
Yes, Calculus welcomes co-investment from accredited investors, family offices, and institutional partners across its portfolio companies. Contact the team at hello@calculusresearch.io to discuss co-investment, licensing, and joint venture opportunities.
