FAQ

Common Questions

What types of deals do you finance?
We focus on hospitality, multifamily real estate, and fintech verticals. This includes bridge loans, construction financing, DSCR rental loans, SBA financing, business lines of credit, and business consulting for growth-stage companies.
How quickly can you close a deal?
Timelines vary by product. Real estate bridge loans typically close in 2-4 weeks depending on complexity and due diligence requirements. SBA loans take 30-60 days. Business lines of credit can be approved in days.
What is the minimum loan amount?
For real estate bridge loans, our minimum is $1M. DSCR rental loans start at $150K. SBA loans are available up to $5.5M. Business lines of credit start at $50K. Business consulting engagements are priced separately based on scope.
Do I need to be an accredited investor?
Accreditation requirements depend on the product. Our management division serves accredited investors and family offices. Lending products have separate qualification criteria based on the asset and deal structure.
Where is Calculus located?
Calculus is headquartered in Providence, Rhode Island, with a regional office in London, United Kingdom. Both offices serve clients across New England and nationally. Office hours are Monday through Friday, 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM Eastern Time.
What industries does Calculus serve?
Calculus serves clients in real estate development (hospitality, multifamily, mixed-use), fintech, private credit, and AI-driven financial services. The firm specializes in bridge loans, DSCR rental loans, SBA financing, business lines of credit, business consulting, and proprietary AI underwriting technology.
