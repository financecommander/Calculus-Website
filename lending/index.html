Frequently Asked Questions

What types of loans does Calculus offer?

Calculus Lending offers seven loan products: Fannie Mae and Freddie Mac agency loans for stabilized multifamily properties ($750K to $100M+ with rates from 5.5% to 7.5%), DSCR rental loans ($150K to $3M with rates from 6.0% to 8.5% and no income verification), USDA B&I guaranteed loans (up to $25M with 80% federal guarantee), real estate bridge loans ($1M to $15M with rates from 9.0% to 12.5%), SBA 7(a) loans (up to $5M at Prime + 2.25%), SBA 504 loans (up to $5.5M at below-market fixed rates), and business lines of credit ($50K to $500K revolving). All products benefit from AI-powered underwriting through the UnderwriteAI engine.

What is a DSCR loan and how does it work?

A DSCR (Debt Service Coverage Ratio) loan is a type of investment property loan that qualifies borrowers based on the rental income of the property rather than the borrower's personal income. At Calculus, DSCR loans range from $150,000 to $3 million per property with 30-year fixed or adjustable-rate terms. The minimum DSCR is as low as 0.75, meaning the property's rental income can be slightly below the mortgage payment and still qualify. No tax returns, W-2s, or income verification is required. These loans are ideal for portfolio investors, LLCs, and real estate professionals who own multiple investment properties.

Does Calculus offer SBA loans?

Yes, Calculus offers both SBA 7(a) and SBA 504 loan programs. SBA 7(a) loans are available up to $5 million for working capital, equipment purchases, and real estate acquisition with 10- to 25-year terms at competitive rates (Prime plus 2.25 to 2.75 percent). SBA 504 loans are available up to $5.5 million for major fixed assets like commercial real estate and heavy equipment with 10- to 25-year fixed terms and below-market rates. Both programs require as little as 10 percent down and are ideal for established businesses with 2 or more years of operating history.

How fast can Calculus close a loan?

Closing timelines at Calculus vary by loan product. Real estate bridge loans close in 2 to 4 weeks. DSCR rental loans typically close in 2 to 4 weeks. Business lines of credit can be approved within days using AI-powered underwriting. SBA loans follow standard SBA processing timelines but benefit from accelerated underwriting through the UnderwriteAI engine. All products are significantly faster than traditional bank financing, which typically takes 60 to 90 days.

Does Calculus offer Fannie Mae and Freddie Mac loans?

Loan amounts range from $750,000 to over $100 million with non-recourse terms, up to 80% LTV, and 5 to 30-year fixed or floating rate options. Current indicative rates range from 5.5% to 7.5%. Agency loans feature 30-year amortization with interest-only periods available and are ideal for large apartment portfolios, workforce housing, and institutional-grade multifamily assets.

What is UnderwriteAI?

UnderwriteAI is Calculus AI Labs' proprietary automated underwriting engine. It analyzes over 700 variables — including DSCR, cap rate, NOI, as-is value, borrower track record, market comparables, and macroeconomic indicators — in under 4 seconds to produce explainable risk scores, deal feasibility assessments, and automated term sheet recommendations. UnderwriteAI uses explainable AI (XAI) with full audit trails and model governance, ensuring transparent and compliant lending decisions across all Calculus loan products.
