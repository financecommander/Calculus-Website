What Is the Calculus AI Portal?

The Calculus AI Portal is a multi-LLM research interface developed by Calculus AI Labs, the research and development division of Calculus, a multi-discipline finance platform headquartered in Boston, Massachusetts. The portal provides institutional clients — including investors, portfolio managers, research partners, and authorized borrowers — with secure, browser-based access to the full suite of Calculus AI products.

Through the portal, authorized users can interact with 16 AI products spanning automated underwriting, alternative credit scoring, market intelligence, settlement infrastructure, voice AI, and quantitative trading models. The platform supports multiple large language models including GPT, Claude, and Gemini alongside proprietary Calculus models such as UnderwriteAI, Voice AI Platform, and Lex Intelligence. All sessions are authenticated via JWT tokens with role-based access controls and encrypted data transmission.

The Calculus AI Portal serves as the primary access point for running live demos of UnderwriteAI, querying Finclusion Score API documentation, exploring MarketPulse market forecasts, and interacting with research prototypes under development. The portal is designed for institutional-grade security and compliance, with full audit trails for every interaction.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Calculus AI Portal?

The Calculus AI Portal is a multi-LLM research platform that provides institutional clients with secure, browser-based access to Calculus AI Labs' full suite of 16 AI products. Users can run live demos of UnderwriteAI, access Finclusion Score API documentation, explore MarketPulse market forecasts, and interact with research prototypes. Authentication is managed via JWT security tokens.

Who can access the Calculus AI Portal?

The AI Portal is available to institutional investors, portfolio managers, research partners, and authorized borrowers. Access is granted upon request by contacting the Calculus team at hello@calculusresearch.io. The platform is designed for institutional-grade use with compliance-ready audit trails.

What AI models are available through the portal?

The portal supports multiple leading large language models including GPT, Claude, and Gemini, alongside proprietary Calculus models such as UnderwriteAI for automated underwriting, Voice AI Platform for conversational AI, and Lex Intelligence for legal document analysis. All 16 AI products in the Calculus AI Labs catalog are accessible through the portal interface.

Is the AI Portal free to use?

Institutional access to the Calculus AI Portal is available upon request at no charge for qualified partners. This includes access to live product demos, API documentation, and research prototypes. Contact hello@calculusresearch.io to request access.

How does the AI Portal handle security?

The Calculus AI Portal uses JWT (JSON Web Token) authentication with role-based access controls. All data transmission is encrypted, and every user interaction generates a full audit trail for compliance and regulatory review. The platform is built for institutional-grade security requirements.
