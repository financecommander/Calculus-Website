What We Do

Business Plan Development

Investor-grade business plans and pitch decks designed to close capital and tell your story with precision.

  • Investor-grade business plans & pitch decks
  • Financial modeling (5-year pro forma, revenue projections, unit economics)
  • Market analysis & competitive positioning

Capital Advisory & Fundraising

End-to-end fundraising support from strategy through close, backed by real capital markets experience.

  • Debt & equity capital raise strategy
  • Investor targeting & introduction
  • Term sheet negotiation & deal structuring
  • Due diligence preparation & data room setup

Fintech & AI Go-to-Market

Launch strategy for fintech and AI companies navigating regulated markets, built by operators who have done it.

  • Product-market fit validation
  • GTM strategy & launch roadmap

Financial modeling and advisory for developers, sponsors, and owner-operators across all asset classes.

  • Entitlement & zoning strategy support
For: Developers, sponsors, and owner-operators

Overview

What Is Calculus Consulting?

Calculus Consulting is the strategic advisory division of Calculus, a multi-discipline finance and technology platform headquartered in Providence, Rhode Island. The consulting practice delivers investor-grade business plans, capital advisory, fintech go-to-market strategy, and real estate development consulting to startups, growth-stage companies, developers, and institutional sponsors. Unlike traditional consulting firms, Calculus Consulting is backed by an active lending and investment platform, meaning every engagement is informed by real capital deployment experience across fintech, real estate, and AI verticals.

FAQ

Consulting Questions

What consulting services does Calculus offer?
Calculus offers four core consulting services: business plan development (investor-grade business plans, pitch decks, and financial modeling), capital advisory and fundraising (debt and equity raise strategy, investor targeting, and term sheet negotiation), fintech and AI go-to-market strategy (product-market fit validation, GTM roadmaps, and regulatory compliance frameworks), and real estate development consulting (pro forma modeling, capital stack structuring, and market feasibility studies). All consulting is informed by our direct experience as active lenders and investors.
How much does Calculus consulting cost?
Calculus consulting engagements are custom-scoped based on the client's needs, timeline, and complexity. Pricing is transparent and provided upfront in a detailed proposal after the initial discovery call. Engagements range from focused deliverables like a single business plan or pitch deck to comprehensive multi-month advisory retainers. Contact the team at hello@calculusresearch.io for a custom quote.
Does Calculus help with fundraising and investor introductions?
Yes. Calculus Capital Advisory helps companies raising between $1 million and $50 million in debt or equity. Services include capital raise strategy, investor targeting and warm introductions, term sheet negotiation, deal structuring, and due diligence preparation including data room setup. The Calculus team has helped clients raise over $150 million in total capital across fintech, real estate, and technology verticals.
What industries does Calculus Consulting serve?
Calculus Consulting serves four primary industry verticals: fintech and digital lending, real estate development (hospitality, multifamily, mixed-use), AI and deep technology companies, and traditional small and mid-size businesses seeking growth capital or strategic advisory. The team has delivered over 50 business plans across these verticals.
Can Calculus help with SBA loan applications?
Yes. Calculus Consulting assists clients with SBA loan preparation, including business plan development, financial projections, and documentation required for SBA 7(a) and 504 loan applications. While Calculus does not originate SBA loans directly, the consulting team ensures that all materials are lender-ready and positioned to meet SBA underwriting requirements.
