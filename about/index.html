Company Overview

About Calculus

Calculus is a multi-discipline finance platform founded in 2020 by Sean Grady in Boston, Massachusetts. The firm operates through three integrated divisions: Calculus Lending (direct lending, loan syndication, and capital advisory), Calculus AI Labs (AI-powered underwriting, machine learning models, and quantitative research), and Calculus Management (institutional-grade discretionary portfolio management for accredited investors and family offices). Since inception, Calculus has deployed more than $150 million in capital across over 120 transactions and manages $40 million or more in assets under management.

The firm's mission centers on three impact-driven pillars: financial inclusion through AI-powered alternative credit scoring for the unbanked and underserved, food security through supply-chain forecasting and agricultural infrastructure financing, and energy stability through funding for resilient energy systems. Calculus serves borrowers, investors, and institutional partners across hospitality, multifamily real estate, and fintech verticals. The company is headquartered in Boston, MA, with additional operations in Windsor, CT.

Calculus AI Labs, the firm's proprietary AI research division launched in 2022, develops machine learning models for deal sourcing, risk analytics, alternative credit scoring, and market forecasting. These AI capabilities power faster underwriting decisions, enabling term sheets within 48 hours and loan closings within two to four weeks. The platform bridges the gap between traditional capital markets and emerging opportunities that legacy institutions cannot efficiently address.
